Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How fast is loan approval?

Pre-approval is usually completed within 24 hours after submitting valid details and documents.

Are there prepayment penalties?

Most plans support early repayment with little or no penalty. Your offer letter will include the exact terms.

What documents are required?

Typically: valid ID, proof of address, bank statement, and income evidence. Business loans may require business registration documents.
