# Privacy Notice (Demo)

## What this site does
This is a frontend-only demo loan website.

## Data you provide
The application form may collect name, email, phone, and loan preferences.

## Where data is stored
- Drafts and application tracker records are stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`.
- Data does not automatically leave your device because there is no backend server.

## Recommendations
- Do not enter real sensitive information on shared/public devices.
- Use the "Clear my data" feature (if available) to remove local data.
